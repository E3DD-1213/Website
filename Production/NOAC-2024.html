National Order of the Arrow Conference - 2024

Seek New Heights

The theme of the National Order of the Arrow Conference in 2024 was "Seek New Heights"

Our team of approximately 130 technicians, designers, actors and directors took over the University of Colorado Events Center, an 11,000 seat arena on the CU Boulder Campus. We worked to build, rehearse and run 4 different themed evening shows over the course of a five-day period for the conference.

As a member of the Audio Team, I worked with the other technicians and designers to deliver a clean, polished mix to the audience. This included both A1 and A2 duties on my part, as well as some sound design and music production for the closing night show.
